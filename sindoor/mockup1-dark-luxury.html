Our Story
35 Years at the Forefront of Asian Fashion

With over 35 years of experience, Sindoor Boutique is at the forefront of Asian Designer fashion. We specialise in bridal, party and casual wear; in addition to exquisite made-to-measure outfits.

Any ready-made item purchased in store can be altered to your specification at no extra cost, usually whilst you wait. A dry cleaning service is also provided in store.
